Homemade Crepes

Whether you're in the mood for a sweet dessert or a French breakfast you can't go wrong with crepes. These crepes are soft and chewy, your stomach won't get enough! Psss Psss don't forget to your chef hat!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all–purpose flour
  • 1.5 Tbsp sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 Tbsp vegetable oil
  • Cooking Spray

Equipment Needed

  • Mixing bowls
  • 10 inch nonstick pan
  • Measuring cup

Crepe Batter – Instructions

  1. Lets start with our dry ingredients! In a bowl we're going to mix our flour and sugar together
  2. In a separate bowl we're going to mix our wet ingredients; add two eggs and one egg yolk, slowly add our vegetable oil while whisking. Finally add our whole milk
  3. Now lets combine both our dry and wet ingredients. Whisk the egg mixture into the flour until you get a smooth texture that's clump–free!

Cooking – Instructions

  1. In a nonstick pan on medium heat spray some cooking oil
  2. Once your pan is hot we're going to add 1/3 of a cup of batter, immediately swirl and rotate the pan around to spread our batter to form an even large circle.
  3. Let it cook until the top starts to get golden brown; flip and cook the other side for 15 seconds.

Time to Serve!

Now if you didn't burn down your kitchen, it's time to eat. These crepes are best served hot and fresh. There's no right way to eat, go crazy with your toppings. My go to toppings included fresh strawberries, bananas, and with a drizzle of lechera. As your beverage you can't go wrong with a smoothie, iced coffee, or a warm cup of milk.
